About Me

My foundation in the biological sciences gave me a unique lens for understanding the complex, dynamic nature of data. While I loved the science, I found myself even more fascinated by the complex data behind it. That curiosity led me to pursue a Master's in Data Science at ASU, where I could focus on building tools that make data useful for everyone, not just experts.

Now, my focus is on building complete, end-to-end solutions. I enjoy the entire process, from wrangling large-scale data using tools like PySpark to developing and deploying sophisticated machine learning models. I’m especially passionate about using technologies like natural language processing to build intelligent systems that are both powerful and intuitive. My goal is always to transform raw data into clear, actionable insights that can drive better decisions.
